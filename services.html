Comprehensive Healthcare for Your Family

At Khatib Family Practice, we offer a wide range of healthcare services to meet the diverse needs of our patients. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, our experienced team provides personalized care for patients of all ages.

We believe in a holistic approach to healthcare, focusing not only on treating illness but also on prevention and maintaining overall wellness. Our goal is to be your trusted healthcare partner throughout every stage of life.

Our Healthcare Services

Primary Care

Our primary care services provide comprehensive healthcare for patients of all ages. We focus on building long-term relationships with our patients to deliver personalized care that addresses your specific health needs.

  • Annual physical examinations
  • Diagnosis and treatment of acute illnesses
  • Management of chronic conditions
  • Health risk assessments
  • Coordination of specialty care

Preventative Care

We believe that preventing illness is just as important as treating it. Our preventative care services are designed to help you maintain optimal health and catch potential health issues before they become serious.

  • Routine health screenings
  • Immunizations for all ages
  • Lifestyle and nutritional counseling
  • Cancer screenings
  • Cardiovascular risk assessments

Pediatric Care

Our pediatric care services focus on the health and development of children from infancy through adolescence. We create a friendly, welcoming environment where children feel comfortable and parents feel informed and supported.

  • Well-child visits
  • Growth and development monitoring
  • Childhood immunizations
  • School and sports physicals
  • Behavioral health assessments

Chronic Disease Management

We provide comprehensive care for patients with chronic conditions, helping you manage your health effectively and improve your quality of life. Our approach focuses on education, medication management, and lifestyle modifications.

  • Diabetes management
  • Hypertension treatment
  • Asthma and COPD care
  • Thyroid disorders
  • Heart disease management

Women's Health

Our women's health services address the unique healthcare needs of women at every stage of life. We provide compassionate, confidential care focused on your overall well-being.

  • Annual well-woman exams
  • Pap smears and cervical cancer screening
  • Family planning services
  • Menopause management
  • Breast health and screenings

Geriatric Care

Our geriatric care services address the complex health needs of older adults. We focus on maintaining quality of life, managing multiple health conditions, and supporting independence.

  • Comprehensive geriatric assessments
  • Management of multiple medications
  • Fall prevention strategies
  • Memory assessments
  • Coordination with specialists and caregivers

Immunizations

We offer a full range of immunizations for patients of all ages, following the recommendations of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

  • Childhood immunization series
  • Adolescent vaccines
  • Adult immunizations
  • Seasonal flu shots
  • Travel vaccines

Telemedicine

Our telemedicine services allow you to consult with our healthcare providers from the comfort of your home. This convenient option is perfect for follow-up appointments, medication management, and certain non-emergency conditions.

  • Video consultations
  • Prescription refills
  • Lab result reviews
  • Minor illness assessment
  • Chronic disease check-ins
