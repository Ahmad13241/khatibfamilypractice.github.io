Appointment Guidelines

  • Please arrive 15 minutes early for your first visit to complete any necessary paperwork.
  • Bring your insurance card and a valid photo ID to each appointment.
  • If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call at least 24 hours in advance.
  • For annual physicals, please fast for 8-12 hours prior to your appointment for accurate lab results.
  • Bring a list of all current medications, including over-the-counter drugs and supplements.
