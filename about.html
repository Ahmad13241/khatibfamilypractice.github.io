Our Mission

At Khatib Family Practice, our mission is to provide exceptional healthcare services that improve the health and well-being of the individuals and families we serve. We are committed to delivering compassionate, comprehensive, and personalized care in a warm and welcoming environment.

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms, and empowering our patients with the knowledge and resources they need to make informed decisions about their health.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We treat each patient with kindness, empathy, and respect, recognizing their unique needs and concerns.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering the highest quality healthcare services and continually improving our knowledge and skills.

Patient-Centered

We place our patients at the center of everything we do, focusing on their needs, preferences, and well-being.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our interactions and decisions, earning the trust of our patients.

Our History

Khatib Family Practice was established in 2005 by Dr. Amir Khatib with a vision to create a healthcare practice that combines medical excellence with a personal touch. What began as a small clinic has grown into a trusted healthcare provider for families in our community.

Over the years, we have expanded our team and services to better meet the diverse needs of our patients, but our commitment to personalized, compassionate care remains unchanged. We are proud of our history and look forward to continuing to serve our community for many years to come.
