Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Completed new patient forms (or arrive 15 minutes early to complete them)
  • Your insurance card and a valid photo ID
  • List of all current medications (including over-the-counter medications and supplements)
  • Medical records from previous providers (if applicable)
  • Any recent lab results or imaging reports
  • Payment for your copay or deductible (we accept cash, check, and major credit cards)

How do I request a prescription refill?

There are several ways to request a prescription refill:

  1. Call our office at (555) 123-4567 and follow the prompts for the prescription refill line
  2. Contact your pharmacy and ask them to send us an electronic refill request
  3. Use our patient portal to request a refill online

Please allow 48 hours for all refill requests to be processed. For controlled substances, please note that you may need to schedule an appointment before a refill can be authorized.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records in several ways:

  1. Through our secure patient portal, which provides 24/7 access to your health information
  2. By completing a Release of Medical Records form and submitting it to our office
  3. By calling our office at (555) 123-4567 to request copies of your records

Please note that there may be a fee for printed copies of medical records, and it may take up to 7-10 business days to process your request.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that situations arise when you may need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. We request that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you cannot keep your scheduled appointment.

Patients who do not show up for their appointment without providing notice may be charged a no-show fee of $25. This fee is not covered by insurance and must be paid before scheduling another appointment.

Multiple no-shows or late cancellations may result in discharge from the practice.

Do you offer telemedicine appointments?

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for certain types of visits, including:

  • Follow-up appointments for chronic conditions
  • Medication management
  • Review of lab results
  • Minor illness assessments

To schedule a telemedicine appointment, please call our office or request one through our patient portal. You will receive instructions on how to join the virtual visit before your scheduled appointment time.

Access Our Patient Portal

Our secure patient portal gives you 24/7 access to your health information and allows you to:

  • View your medical records and lab results
  • Request prescription refills
  • Send secure messages to our healthcare team
  • View upcoming appointments and request new ones
  • Update your personal and insurance information
  • Pay your bill online
